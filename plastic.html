Recycling of plastics

Final products of recycled plastics, that are useful for human consumption.

Plastics can be recycled by melting and shaped into different forms of other useful products like cups, spoons, plates, pipes among others.
Such items are durable, strong, lightweight, inexpensive to manufacture as it doesn't requuire much capital and become purposeful in our day-to-day lives.
In the healthcare sectors, plastics can be formed into syringes, gloves, plastic bandages and containers where other tools are stored and prevent contamination and infection control.
In Kenya, there is a ban of use of plastic polythene bags as they are non-biodegradable, and people have been encouraged to use the 'uhuru bags', as they are more eco-friendly.

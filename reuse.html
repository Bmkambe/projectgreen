Reuse

Plastic jerican when old can be used as a pot for growing plants.

Reusing plastic waste that is non-biodegradable in creative ways is being encouraged by many companies and activities.
These materials are mostly used in the agricultural sector, where holes are bore into the bottles and a pipe is passed through it to allow water to sprinkle in all directions in the farm.
Some may use plastic bottles as holders for their crops or flowers.
Farmers may also apply the aerial method of farming, which is advantageous as the plants acquire enough elements it requires for it to flourish.
These plastics are used to contain and conserve water in farms.
Poeple have found creative ways of using these plastics even in their homes, as they play the role of flower vases. Coca-cola company is encouraging people to reuse their plastic bottles for othe rpurposes other than disposing them off incorrectly.
